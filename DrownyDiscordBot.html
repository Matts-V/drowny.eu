Drowny Music

Drowny Music

With Drowny Music you can play music from any service you think of.
Youtube, Spotify, Soundcloud Just name it! it supports almost every URL.

Commands

/clear - Clear all tracks from queue
/filters - add or remove filters
/help - Shows this list
/invite - Invite me to your server
/loop - Loops the current song
/loopq - Loop the current song queue
/lyrics - Get the lyrics of a song
/move - Moves track to a different position
/nowplaying - Shows the song currently playing in the voice channel.
/pause - Pauses the current playing track
/play - Searches and plays the requested song
/queue -  Shows the current queue
/reload - Reload all commands
/remove - Remove track you don't want from queue
/resume - Resume current track
/rickroll - Rickroll your call
/save - Saves current song to your DM's
/search - Search for a song
/seek - Seek to a specific time in the current song.
/shuffle - Randomizes the queue
/skip - Skip the current song
/skipto - skip to a specific song in the queue
/stats - Get information about the bot
/stop - Stops whatever the bot is playing and leaves the voice channel
/summon - Summons the bot to the channel.
/volume - Change the volume of the current song.
